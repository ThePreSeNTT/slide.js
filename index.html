slide.js

turn your content into a slideshow

slide.js is a jQuery based script that allows to turn contents of your website or blog post into a slideshow that runs inside a browser.

This allows you to share your presentation with others as a nicely printable website, not just as a pack of slides.

How does it work?

It's actually no much more than than switching the CSS styles. When slide show mode is turned on the <body> element gets the .slideshow class, so you can easily define rules in your CSS file.
